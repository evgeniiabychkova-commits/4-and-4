4/4 is an independent project label operating at the intersection
of sound, visual culture, and spatial experience. Inspired by the repetitive structures of early underground electronic music and the perceptual experimentation of psychedelic graphic design, 4/4 creates temporary environments where sound, image, and architecture become part of a single system. Each event is conceived as a unique experience, bringing together musicians, visual artists, designers, and audiences within unconventional urban spaces.
